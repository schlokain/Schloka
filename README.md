## About Us

Schloka.in is a pan-India classified directory platform that connects users with verified service providers across all major cities. We offer a safe, private, and user-friendly browsing experience.

## What We Offer

- **Verified Profiles** — Every listing goes through a verification process
- **Pan India Coverage** — Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Chennai & more
- **Free Listings** — Anyone can list their services for free
- **Premium Options** — Boost visibility with premium membership
- **User Privacy** — Your safety and privacy is our top priority
